Purandar Fort

Purandar Fort, located near Pune in Maharashtra, is a historic fort with immense significance in Maratha history. It is situated at an altitude of 4,472 feet and offers panoramic views of the surrounding region.

The fort is closely associated with Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj and is known as the birthplace of his son, Sambhaji Maharaj. It served as a strategic stronghold during the Maratha Empire and played a key role in various battles.

Purandar Fort is divided into two parts: the upper fort, known as "Balekilla," and the lower fort. The fort features ancient temples, water reservoirs, and gateways that reflect its historical importance.

Today, Purandar Fort is a popular destination for trekkers, history enthusiasts, and nature lovers. Its historical significance and scenic beauty make it a must-visit site in Maharashtra.
