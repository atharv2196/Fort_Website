Shivneri Fort

Shivneri Fort, located near Junnar in Maharashtra, is a historic fort renowned as the birthplace of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, the founder of the Maratha Empire. It is perched atop a hill and offers stunning views of the surrounding region.

The fort is known for its strong fortifications, ancient water reservoirs, and historical structures, including the Shivai Devi Temple. It served as a strategic stronghold during the Maratha Empire and played a significant role in history.

Shivneri Fort is accessible via a well-maintained trekking route and features seven massive gates that lead to the top. The fort's architecture and historical significance make it a popular destination for history enthusiasts and trekkers.

Today, Shivneri Fort stands as a symbol of Maratha pride and heritage. Its historical importance and scenic beauty make it a must-visit site in Maharashtra.
